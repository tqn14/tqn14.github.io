Analysis of drilling operations in Oil and Gas industry

This project is a modern data driven approach to engineering issues, with the goal of using data to build models that can detect several drilling dysfunctions such as abnormal vibrations, and identify different lithofacies of the wells. This will be helpful by cutting the cost of consultation and automatically deriving a proper drilling plan for the operations. The high frequency real-time dataset is obtained from a major oil and gas company. Several Machine Learning algorithms (among regression, clustering, classification, the Recurrent Neural Networks method was mainly concerned) were applied in different models and yield reliable results.

We are a team of six interdisciplinary students working together. More information about the project can be found here.
