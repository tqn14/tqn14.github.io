Analysis of drilling operations in Oil and Gas industry

A project in Dr. Caverlee class. With a dataset obtained from the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, we assigned an efficiency score for each airport in the US, based on their cancellation rate and delay rate.

Through this project, we hope to provide customers with some insight so that they can plan their travelling, for example, by choosing an overlay airport with short wait-time and better airport amenities. It can also be beneficial to airports themselves by having a reference to make changes to their operations, organizations and architectural designs.
